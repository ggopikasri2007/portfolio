About Me

My name is Gopika. I have completed my 12th standard and come from a middle-class family. Due to financial difficulties, my father was unable to afford my college education. At that time, my brother told me about Freshworks Software Academy, and I applied. I was fortunate to get selected for the program.

My dream is to become a full-stack developer, build a successful career, and make my parents proud.
